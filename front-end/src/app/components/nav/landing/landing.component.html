Ashen Skill list

Thank you for taking the time to have a look!

What is this?

This project is a way to collect and get an overview of the various skills that soldiers in the Chapter have. This also establishes what skills one learns during basic trainings.

Why?

Officers and non-commisioned officers can use this application to make more specific, targeted trainings. If, for example, they see that few people are able to do a certain skill, trainings can be held for those specific people to improve specific skills. It also allows, in certain scenario's, to quickly pick the right person to do on a job.

The choice to make a seperate application for this, instead of using something like Google Sheets or Forms, was made due to three important factors to us:

  1. Anonimity
    We didn't want you to have to use an email address or anything else that you wouldn't want to share. Now you can use only your account name or Enjin-handle, and that will be enough.
  2. Only being able to look at your own answers
    We wanted to keep the answers separate on a person by person basis. You should only be able to look at your own answers.
  3. Being able to change your answers
    We wanted to be sure that you can update your answers as your character grows and gets more training.

Finally, how does it work?

There are a few steps to this whole process:

  1. Create an account. You will be asked to fill in a name (can be anything, but we would prefer it to be something we can use to identify you with.)
  2. Create a character. Because you can have 2 characters in the guild, you can fill in answers for all of your characters.
  3. Fill in answers for your character. After you've done this, we will be able to look at in an overview only the officers have access to.

Start with creating your account or go to the character overview!
